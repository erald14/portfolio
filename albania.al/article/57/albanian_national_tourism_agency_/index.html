NTA of Albania

The Albanian National Tourism Agency carries out its activity in support of and implementation of the Constitution of the Law No.93 / 2015, "On Tourism", DCM no. 298, date 20.04.2016 and other legal and sub legal acts in force. The National Tourism Agency, here in after "ACP", is a public entity with the status of legal, public, budgetary entity, functions as an institution under the Minister responsible for tourism.
